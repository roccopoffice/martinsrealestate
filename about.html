About our company

The qualified and experienced agents at Martins & Associates Real Estate know that real estate isn’t just about prices; it’s also about relationships. Our CEO, Jeanie Martins, began her real estate journey in 1997 and has made a reputable name for herself as well as her business. Since 2003, when she decided to open her own firm, she and her team have continued to match our clients in Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and surrounding areas with properties that fit their lifestyle and budget.

We understand that buying or selling a home isn’t just a business transaction; it’s a whole new beginning. That’s why we offer an extensive array of services, as well as the human touch, making the process as easy and hassle-free as possible. Book online or call us to get started today!

“Real estate isn’t just about prices—it’s about people, timing, and trust built over years.” Martins & Associates

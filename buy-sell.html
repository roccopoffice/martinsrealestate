How buying works with us

A clear path from first conversation to closing—so you always know what comes next.

  1. Consultation & financing We align on budget, timeline, and neighborhoods, and help you connect with trusted lenders when you are ready to get pre-approved.
  2. Search & showings You get listings that match your criteria, private tours, and honest feedback on value and condition.
  3. Offer & negotiation We structure a competitive offer, negotiate terms, and explain contingencies in plain language.
  4. Inspections & due diligence From home inspections to additional visits, we coordinate access and keep the deal on track.
  5. Mortgage & legal We stay in sync with your lender and attorney so paperwork and deadlines do not slip.
  6. Closing Final walkthrough, signing, and keys—then we celebrate your new chapter.

Have a specific town or property type in mind? Tell us what you are looking for and we will start the search with you.

How we sell your home

A focused plan to prepare, price, and market your property so the right buyers see it at the right time.

  1. Pricing & strategy We review comparable sales, condition, and timing to set a price that attracts attention without leaving money on the table.
  2. Prep & presentation From simple staging suggestions to contractor referrals, we help your home show at its best.
  3. Marketing & exposure Professional photos, listings, and targeted outreach bring qualified buyers and serious offers.
  4. Showings & feedback We coordinate tours, follow up with buyers’ agents, and adjust tactics as needed.
  5. Offers & negotiation We compare offers with you, negotiate terms, and manage deadlines through inspection and financing.
  6. Closing We coordinate with your attorney and title company through a smooth transfer of ownership.

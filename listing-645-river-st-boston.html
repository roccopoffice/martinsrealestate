645 River St is a redevelopment opportunity in Hyde Park: a vision to replace the existing grocery use with a new three-story mixed-use building. The first floor is planned for roughly 2,000 sq ft of commercial space suited to a self-service laundry, serving neighborhood demand. Two upper floors would deliver four residential units (two per floor) for a strong blend of retail and rental income.

The site offers visibility along River Street, access to public transportation, and public water and sewer — a practical base for an infill project in a busy Boston neighborhood. Buyers and developers should confirm all entitlements, plans, and permitted uses with the City of Boston and their own counsel.
